Frequently Asked Questions

Who can benefit from these exercises?

Our exercises are designed for individuals recovering from stroke, brain injury, and other neurological conditions affecting motor skills. They can be used as part of a home exercise program or under the guidance of a therapist.

How often should I use these exercises?

We recommend consulting with your healthcare provider for personalized guidance. Generally, short but frequent sessions (15-30 minutes, 3-5 times per week) are more beneficial than occasional long sessions.

1. Introduction

Fine Point Rehab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our rehabilitation exercises.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our website. By accessing or using our website, you agree to this privacy policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect very limited information from users of our website:

  • Personal Information: When you contact us through our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as which exercises you use, the settings you select, and your performance metrics (e.g., scores, completion times) is stored locally on your device using localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Basic Device Information: Some standard information about your browser and device is automatically collected by our web server when you visit our site, such as your IP address, which is used to maintain the security and operation of our website.

We do not collect health information or medical data. The exercises are provided as tools for general use, and any performance data is stored only on your device and not accessible to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our website and exercises
  • To respond to your inquiries if you contact us
  • To protect our website from unauthorized access or activities
  • To comply with any applicable legal obligations

Since most of your exercise data is stored only on your device, we do not use this information for analytics, profiling, or any other purposes.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. The only circumstances in which we might share information are:

  • Service Providers: We may share basic information with service providers who help us operate our website (such as website hosting providers).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Since your exercise data and preferences are stored locally on your device, we do not have access to this information and therefore cannot share it with third parties.

5. Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage, which is a technology that stores data locally on your device rather than on our servers. We use localStorage to:

  • Remember your exercise settings and preferences
  • Store your performance data (scores, completion times, etc.)
  • Provide a personalized experience without requiring user accounts

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. It persists until you clear your browser's localStorage or use your browser's privacy settings to delete it.

Most web browsers allow you to view and delete localStorage data through their privacy or security settings. Please refer to your browser's documentation for specific instructions.

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical measures to protect the security of the limited information we collect. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Our security measures include:

  • Use of secure HTTPS protocol for our website
  • Regular security assessments and updates

Since most of your data is stored locally on your device, the security of this information also depends on your device's security measures.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information in the following ways:

  • Local Data: You can clear your localStorage data through your browser settings at any time.
  • Communication: If you have contacted us and wish to have your information deleted, you can request this by emailing us.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and exercises are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@finepointrehab.com

We will make every effort to respond to your inquiry promptly and address your concerns.

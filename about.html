About Fine Point Rehab

Fine Point Rehab was founded with a simple mission: to make high-quality rehabilitation exercises accessible to everyone who needs them, regardless of location or financial resources.

Our interactive digital exercises are designed by a team of therapists. We combine clinical expertise with engaging digital experiences to create tools that are both effective and enjoyable to use.

Our Mission

To improve the lives of individuals with a range of conditions by providing evidence-based, accessible rehabilitation tools that enhance motor skills, coordination, and cognitive function.

Our Vision

A world where anyone recovering from stroke, brain injury, or other neurological conditions has access to high-quality, engaging rehabilitation exercises that help them regain independence and improve quality of life.

Our Values

  • Evidence-Based Practice: All our exercises are grounded in scientific research and clinical expertise.
  • Accessibility: We believe rehabilitation resources should be available to everyone who needs them.
  • Engagement: Rehabilitation is most effective when it's engaging and motivating.
  • Personalization: Each person's rehabilitation journey is unique and deserves customized support.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with healthcare professionals and users to continuously improve our tools.

Our Story

Fine Point Rehab began when our founders, two Occupational Therapists with over 25 years of combined experience, recognized that many patients struggled to maintain their exercise regimens at home. Traditional paper handouts were often lost or forgotten, and many patients found the exercises repetitive and uninspiring.

By partnering with digital experts and fellow rehabilitation specialists, we developed a suite of interactive exercises that turned traditional rehabilitation movements into engaging digital experiences. What started as a simple concept has grown into a comprehensive platform that serves thousands of users worldwide.

Research & Evidence

Fine Point Rehab is committed to evidence-based practice. Our exercises are designed based on established principles of motor learning, neuroplasticity, and rehabilitation science.

Recent studies have shown that gamified rehabilitation exercises can significantly improve adherence to home exercise programs, with corresponding improvements in functional outcomes. Our own internal data suggests that users engage with our digital exercises 3-4 times more frequently than with traditional paper handouts.
